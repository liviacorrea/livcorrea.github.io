Research Topics

Thermal Management of Solid State Electronics

One of the biggest challenges on the design of modern electronic devices is the thermal control. Because the dimensions of modern solid-state electronics (SSE) keep decreasing, the necessity to dissipate the heat and to keep the temperature under their ideal operational ranges have boosted several studies about thermal management. This research is focused in obtain analytical solutions utilizing the Classical Integral Transform Technique (CITT) to analyze heat transfer in Solid-State Electronics (SSE) and heat sinks (HS) for electronic cooling.
