Research overview

Research interests include:

  • Near-field radiative heat transfer in micro-nano structured materials
  • Nano-optics applied to near-field radiative heat transfer
  • Analytical and numerical methodologies for heat transfer analysis

Near-field radiative heat transfer between thin membranes

This research aims to analyze the physics of near-field radiative heat transfer (NFRHT) between thin membranes of variable size separated by sub-wavelength vacuum gaps. For that purpose, NFRHT between 3D finite membranes is calculated via the discrete system Green’s function method, a numerical framework based on fluctuational electrodynamics. This research finds application in non-contact thermal management of micro/nanoelectronic devices.

A numerical framework for near-field radiative heat transfer calculations

Based on the discrete system Green's function [Walter et al., 2022], a comprehensive numerical framework for near-field radiative heat transfer (NFRHT) is developed. Two versions in different languages (MATLAB and c99) are available in GitHub.

Thermal management of solid-state electronics

This project proposed an analytical approach to estimate the temperature field in solid state electronics (SSE). The formulation considers several internal heat generation (HG) regions caused by Joule effect due to internal components, which can be applied in any layout of internal components in the SSE. In order to obtain the analytical solution, the Classical Integral Transform Technique was used and verified using Finite Difference Method.

Thermal analysis of heat sinks

In this research, the thermal analysis of heat sinks, used for solid-state electronics cooling, is proposed using an analytical approach. Analytical solutions for the base and fins are obtained using the Classical Integral Transform Technique, and coupled using eigenfunction expansion to obtain the temperature field for the heat sink. Computational fluid dynamics simulations using OpenFOAM software are also performed for verification purposes.
