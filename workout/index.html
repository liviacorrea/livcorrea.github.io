This website was created to list my favorite workout resources and access them easily.

Self-care is an important part of my life, and working out is in its foundation. I have been working out in a gym since 2011.
In a new country, I started going to new classes at the Student Life Center of the University of Utah.
In 2024, I became a HIGH fitness and HIGH LOW instructor.

HIGH fitness mixes aerobic training and anaerobic training with active recovery tracks to create an interval training effect.
HIGH LOW has the same high energy and intensity from HIGH fitness, but having a steady state cardio with less impact on joints.

For strength training, I follow the full-body workout below with variations.
The variations are listed into tabs targeting the different parts of the body.
Legs, arms, abs, thighs, glutes...don't forget to warm-up!
